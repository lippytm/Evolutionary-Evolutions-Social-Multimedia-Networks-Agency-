# 🤝 Partnerships & Sponsorships — Evolutionary Evolutions Agency

## Overview

Strategic partnerships and sponsorships are among the fastest paths to revenue and audience growth. This document provides outreach templates, pricing guidance, and a list of ideal partner categories.

---

## 🎯 Ideal Partner Profile

We seek partners who share our values and serve our audience of developers, makers, and business builders.

**Perfect fit for us:**
- Developer tools companies (IDEs, cloud platforms, CI/CD)
- Blockchain and Web3 projects seeking developer mindshare
- Online learning platforms and bootcamps
- Hardware companies (robotics kits, Raspberry Pi, Arduino)
- Crypto exchanges and wallets targeting developers
- Productivity and software tools

## 📧 Outreach Email Templates

### Template 1 — Cold Outreach to Developer Tool Company

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity — [Your Agency Name] × [Company Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], founder of Evolutionary Evolutions — a multimedia and
technology agency focused on blockchain development, robotics, and
AI-powered education for developers.

Our GitHub repositories collectively reach thousands of developers
interested in [relevant tech], which aligns perfectly with [Company]'s
target audience.

I'd love to explore a content partnership where we:
- Feature [Company]'s tools in our tutorials and video series
- Create sponsored content tailored to your developer audience
- Include [Company] in our weekly newsletter (X subscribers)

Sponsorship packages start at $[X]/month. I'm happy to share our
audience stats and discuss a custom fit.

Would you have 20 minutes for a quick call?

Best,
[Your Name]
[GitHub Profile / Website]
```

### Template 2 — Warm Outreach (Already a User)

```
Subject: Love [Product] — Would You Be Open to a Partnership?

Hi [Name],

I'm a big fan of [Product] — I use it daily for [specific use case]
and recommend it regularly to my developer community.

I run Evolutionary Evolutions, a GitHub-based agency covering
blockchain, robotics, and developer education. We'd love to feature
[Company] as an official partner in our content.

In exchange, I'd offer:
- Sponsored content slot in our tutorial series (X monthly readers)
- Logo placement in our top repositories
- Social media shoutouts to X followers

Open to a quick conversation?

[Your Name]
```

### Template 3 — Grant / Ecosystem Fund Application

```
Subject: Grant Application — [Project Name] — Evolutionary Evolutions

Hi [Grants Team],

I'm applying for a [Foundation Name] grant to support [Project Name],
an open-source [description] built for the [ecosystem] developer community.

Our project:
- Solves: [problem]
- For: [target audience]
- Current status: [repo link, stars, contributors]
- Requested grant: $[amount]
- Timeline: [X weeks/months]

We are committed to open-source development and community education,
which aligns with [Foundation]'s mission of [their mission].

Full proposal attached / available at [link].

Thank you for your consideration,
[Your Name]
```

## 📌 Where to Find Sponsors

1. **GitHub Sponsors** — Developers and companies already using GitHub
2. **Dev.to sponsors** — Brands already spending on developer content
3. **Open Collective** — For open-source funding and transparency
4. **Product Hunt** — Many new products looking for early adopters
5. **AngelList / Crunchbase** — Identify funded startups targeting developers
6. **LinkedIn** — Search "Developer Relations", "Dev Advocacy", "Open Source" job titles for decision makers
